About Us INTRO How to drive a Battlebot

In order to drive a Battlebot you must:

1. Connect to the Battlebot wifi network. 2. Navigate to the control page. 3. Try not to crash! NOTES: If the Battlebot begins to beep, disconnect and charge the battery immediately. Application set permanently to 192.168.100.1:9999. This application supports bluetooth controllers. RESOURCES Our Website Our Github Our Thingiverse BattleBot ChatRoom Overide to Control
